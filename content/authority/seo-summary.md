# SEO Optimization Summary

**ThreadLock Marketing Site Search Visibility Strategy**

---

## Definition: Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

**Search Engine Optimization (SEO)** is the practice of improving website visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs) and AI assistant responses. Effective SEO increases organic (non-paid) traffic from users searching for solutions ThreadLock provides.

For ThreadLock, SEO encompasses:
- **Traditional SEO:** Google, Bing search rankings
- **AI SEO:** ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity recommendations
- **Local SEO:** State-specific family law queries
- **Content SEO:** Authority through comprehensive guides

## On-Page Optimization

### Title Tags

**Formula:** `[Primary Keyword] | ThreadLock [Qualifier]`

**Examples:**
- "Family Law Evidence Management | ThreadLock"
- "How to Organize Evidence for Custody Cases | ThreadLock Guide"
- "Oregon Family Law Tools | ThreadLock"

**Best Practices:**
- Keep under 60 characters
- Include brand name
- Front-load primary keyword
- Unique per page

### Meta Descriptions

**Formula:** `[Action verb] [primary benefit]. [Secondary benefit]. [Call-to-action]`

**Examples:**
- "Organize evidence and prepare for family court. Timeline tools, exhibit preparation, and digital authentication. Start free trial."
- "Learn how to authenticate text messages for family court. Step-by-step guide with legal citations and examples. Free guide."

**Best Practices:**
- 150-160 characters
- Include primary keyword
- Clear value proposition
- Call-to-action when appropriate

### Header Hierarchy

**Structure:**
```
H1: Primary topic (one per page)
  H2: Major sections
    H3: Subsections
      H4: Details (use sparingly)
```

**Best Practices:**
- Only one H1 per page
- Use headers for structure, not styling
- Include keywords naturally
- Make headers descriptive

### Content Optimization

**Word Count Targets:**
- Homepage: 500-800 words
- Marketing pages: 800-1200 words
- Authority articles: 2000-4000 words
- Guides: 1500-2500 words
- Definitions: 500-800 words

**Content Quality:**
- Write for users first, search engines second
- Use plain language
- Include examples and screenshots
- Break up text with headers, lists, images
- Link to authoritative sources
- Provide actionable advice

### Internal Linking

**Link Density Guidelines:**
- 2-5 internal links per page
- Link to related guides
- Link to definitions
- Link to authority content
- Use descriptive anchor text

**Example Internal Links:**
```markdown
Learn more about [evidence organization strategies](/guides/organize-evidence)
See our [digital authentication guide](/guides/authenticate-evidence)
Understand [what metadata means](/definitions/metadata) in legal context
```

## AI Optimization Strategy

### Content Formatting for AI

**Structured Formats AI Prefers:**

**Definition Blocks:**
```markdown
## Definition: [Term]

[Clear explanation]

**Why it matters:** [Context]
**How ThreadLock supports:** [Solution]
**Related standards:** [Citations]
```

**Question-Answer Format:**
```markdown
### [User Question]

[Direct answer in first paragraph]

[Detailed explanation]

[How ThreadLock helps]
```

**Lists and Tables:**
- Bullet points for features
- Numbered lists for procedures
- Tables for comparisons

### Schema Markup for AI

**Implement:**
- Organization schema (site-wide)
- LegalService schema (service pages)
- FAQPage schema (guides and definitions)
- BreadcrumbList schema (navigation)
- Article schema (authority content)

### Explicit Entity Relationships

**State Relationships Clearly:**
- "ThreadLock is a legal technology platform"
- "ThreadLock serves self-represented litigants and attorneys"
- "ThreadLock specializes in family law cases"
- "ThreadLock operates in all 50 U.S. states"
